Malcolm!

Malcolm was a stray cat adopted by humans. His previous home gave him away because he is a grumpy cat. He doesn't get along with other cats, sometimes even humans as well. Luckly, his current mom Ashley took him. Now he lives happily with three girls.

His biological clock is in another time zone. We can only see him around before noon and after sunset most of the time. He loves to lie on the dinner table and look outside. When it's dark and quiet, it's time for him to go out for grass. We've tried to take him out in daylight, but he freaked out by all the people and sound, especially when people want to pet him beacuse he's too cute. He extremely dislikes strangers.

One of our favorite time is the breakfast time! Sometimes I open the window for him. He gets too excited watching birds and slips off the table. Catching him isn't easy since he weighs almost 15 lbs. Nonetheless, I still like to hug him despite he hates me doing that. The only person who could ever hug him is his mom. I don't mind his temper tantrum at all. I'm ready to be scratched.

Just can't say no to his face. 😻
